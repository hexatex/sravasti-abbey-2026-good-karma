# Memorial day weekend retreat 2026 unofficial guide
My unofficial at home guide for Sravasti Abbeys 2026 Memorial Day Weekend "Good Karma" retreat, happening from May 22 to May 25.

https://sravastiabbey.org/event/good-karma-memorial-day-weekend-retreat-2026/

## Materials
* Meditation tracks from this guide: https://www.shambhala.com/guidedbuddhistmeditations/
* Good Karma by Thubten Chodron
	* Buy the book if you can, it's great.
	* If you cannot there are limited digital copies available here: https://archive.org/details/goodkarmahowtocr0000thub/page/n15/mode/2up

## Pre-Retreat Preparation
* Get a copy of Good Karma by Venerable Chodron
* Read up to verse 45 in preparation for the retreat. That is page 104 in my copy.
	* This will get you caught up
	* Alternately you can watch all previous retreat videos
* Clean your room
* Make sure you are waking up somewhere between 4 and 5 am days in advance so you are not sleepy
* Practice your meditation in advance
* Cook 4 days worth of food in advance, just before it starts.
* Install "Insight Timer" app on your phone
	* Create "retreat morning meditation" timer preset: 1h 30m duration, with a 1h 20m interval
	* Create "retreat analytical meditation" timer preset: 55m duration, with no interval
* (optional) read chapter 6 of the second book in the library of wisdom and compassion, especially the section "The Actual Session and Dedication at the Conclusion"

## At Home schedule
An at home version of the retreat schedule.

If things do go to plan, disturbances occur: practice patience, compassion, equanimity! It's alright, it doesn't have to go perfectly!! Be grateful to any disturbances and the opportunity to practice that they present.

`4:30pm`

## Morning Meditation - 5:30am - 1 hour 30 minute
Follow the guided meditations online

* Meditation on the Buddha (Track A2)
* Breath meditation track (Track A3)
* Next Lamrim analysis track (Track B1-G9 what ever is next for you)
* Resting meditation until first bell
* Metta until final bell - 10 minutes
* Dedication track

## Live Streamed Lesson
* If you have time in advance pre-read the upcoming verses and commentary in Good Karma
* Take notes, points are for analyses in the meditation.
* At the end review your notes in preparation for the meditation session

## Post-Lesson Meditation - 11am & 7pm - 55 minutes
A Lamrim style analytical meditation, using some of the guided meditation tracks.

* Short Recitations (Track A4)
* Analysis on the lesson regarding the wheel of sharp weapons - as long as it takes to produce strong feeling of insight
* Rest in the understanding produced by your insight until 55 minute bell rings
* Dedication (Track A5)
